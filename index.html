About Me

  • 2017-2018

    My Beginnings

    I am Nonzulu Kala ,I have completed a one year programme at Project CodeX, in agile fullstack web development. My passion is all about providing solutions to problems ,creating real elegant looking web applications. Keeping it simple and clean with added functionality of user interaction. We learn't JavaScript , Node.JS, Handlebar.JS, Express.JS, MongoDB, Test Driven Development(TDD) using mocha, HTML, CSS, Git and Agile principles of development such as Sprint planning, daily Standups, demonstration and Retrospective. Every end of the term we did 4 days Hackathon where we built solutions for problems presented by our sponsor such as Jumo and Steltix.
